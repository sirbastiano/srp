OPERATOR: SubGraph
----------------------------------------
Usage:
  gpt SubGraph [options] 

Description:
  Encapsulates a graph within a graph.


Parameter Options:
  -PgraphFile=<file>    Sets parameter 'graphFile' to <file>.

Graph XML Format:
  <graph id="someGraphId">
    <version>1.0</version>
    <node id="someNodeId">
      <operator>SubGraph</operator>
      <sources/>
      <parameters>
        <graphFile>file</graphFile>
      </parameters>
    </node>
  </graph>
