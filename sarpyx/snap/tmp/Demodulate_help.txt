OPERATOR: Demodulate
----------------------------------------
Usage:
  gpt Demodulate [options] 

Description:
  Demodulation and deramping of SLC data


Source Options:
  -SsourceProduct=<file>    Sets source 'sourceProduct' to <filepath>.
                            This is a mandatory source.

  <graph id="someGraphId">
    <version>1.0</version>
    <node id="someNodeId">
      <operator>Demodulate</operator>
      <sources>
        <sourceProduct>${sourceProduct}</sourceProduct>
      </sources>
      <parameters/>
    </node>
  </graph>
