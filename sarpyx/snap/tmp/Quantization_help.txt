OPERATOR: Quantization
----------------------------------------
Usage:
  gpt Quantization [options] 

Description:
  Converts each pixel from a band based on a lookup table


Source Options:
  -Ssource=<file>    The product which contains the raster.
                     This is a mandatory source.

Parameter Options:
  -PbandName=<string>     The band to convert.
  -PintervalsMap=<map>    The intervals used for conversion, separated by semicolons.

Graph XML Format:
  <graph id="someGraphId">
    <version>1.0</version>
    <node id="someNodeId">
      <operator>Quantization</operator>
      <sources>
        <source>${source}</source>
      </sources>
      <parameters>
        <bandName>string</bandName>
        <intervalsMap>map</intervalsMap>
      </parameters>
    </node>
  </graph>
