OPERATOR: SnaphuImport
----------------------------------------
Usage:
  gpt SnaphuImport [options] 

Description:
  Ingest SNAPHU results into InSAR product.


Parameter Options:
  -PdoNotKeepWrapped=<boolean>    Sets parameter 'doNotKeepWrapped' to <boolean>.
                                  Default value is 'false'.

        <doNotKeepWrapped>boolean</doNotKeepWrapped>
      </parameters>
    </node>
