OPERATOR: ChangeVectorAnalysisOp
----------------------------------------
Usage:
  gpt ChangeVectorAnalysisOp [options] 

Description:
  The 'Change Vector Analysis' between two dual bands at two differents dates.


Source Options:
  -SsourceProduct1=<file>    The sources product at the first date.
                             This is a mandatory source.
  -SsourceProduct2=<file>    The sources product at the second date.
                             This is a mandatory source.

Parameter Options:
  -PmagnitudeThreshold=<string>    No change detection magnitude threshold
                                   Default value is '0'.
  -PsourceBand1=<string>           Band 1 at the same date
  -PsourceBand2=<string>           Band 2 at the same date

Graph XML Format:
  <graph id="someGraphId">
    <version>1.0</version>
    <node id="someNodeId">
      <operator>ChangeVectorAnalysisOp</operator>
      <sources>
        <sourceProduct1>${sourceProduct1}</sourceProduct1>
        <sourceProduct2>${sourceProduct2}</sourceProduct2>
      </sources>
      <parameters>
        <sourceBand1>string</sourceBand1>
        <sourceBand2>string</sourceBand2>
        <magnitudeThreshold>string</magnitudeThreshold>
      </parameters>
    </node>
  </graph>
