OPERATOR: Raster-To-Vector
----------------------------------------
Usage:
  gpt Raster-To-Vector [options] 

Description:
  Converts a raster band to a vector


Source Options:
  -Ssource=<file>    The product which contains the raster.
                     This is a mandatory source.

Parameter Options:
  -PbandName=<string>    The band to convert.

Graph XML Format:
  <graph id="someGraphId">
    <version>1.0</version>
    <node id="someNodeId">
      <operator>Raster-To-Vector</operator>
      <sources>
        <source>${source}</source>
      </sources>
      <parameters>
        <bandName>string</bandName>
      </parameters>
    </node>
  </graph>
